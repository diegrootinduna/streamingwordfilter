# Streaming Word Filter

**A free Chrome extension that quietly mutes specific words — profanity, blasphemy, or anything you choose — in real time on your favourite streaming services.**

## Why this exists

I'm a Christian dad, and I got tired of wincing every time a word came up in a show I was watching with my daughter. Pausing, skipping back, covering her ears — it was exhausting. And I'd grown numb to language I didn't actually want to grow numb to.

So I built Streaming Word Filter. It watches the captions on your favourite streaming services, catches the words you've chosen to filter before you hear them, and adds a brief visual overlay so the moment passes without the jolt. You keep watching. Nothing else changes.

It's built for families and faith-based households who want cleaner viewing without giving up the shows they enjoy.

## How it works

1. Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store
2. Open the popup and add words to your filter list (or use a preset)
3. Turn on captions on your streaming service — or let the extension enable them for you
4. Press play

When a filtered word appears in the captions, the extension mutes the audio and applies a brief visual overlay. A moment later, playback continues as normal.

- HBO Max / Max

The extension works on most services that support closed captions. If a platform stops working due to a streaming service update, please [open an issue](../../issues) and I'll fix it.

## Features

- **Real-time filtering** — reads captions and mutes audio the moment a filtered word is detected
- **Custom word lists** — add your own words, or use preset lists for profanity and blasphemous terms
- **Auto-enable captions** — the extension can turn on captions for you on supported platforms
- **Customisable overlay** — adjust colour, opacity, and duration
- **Private by design** — no account, no tracking, no data leaves your device
- **Lightweight** — only active on supported streaming sites

## Privacy

Streaming Word Filter does not collect, transmit, or share any data. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your filter list and preferences are stored using Chrome's built-in extension storage.

Read the full [Privacy Policy](https://diegrootinduna.github.io/familystreamfilter/privacy.html).

## Installation

### From the Chrome Web Store (recommended)

[Install Streaming Word Filter](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/streaming-service-word-fi/fpdahjohhcohefpobeiojeckjdadgodp) with one click.

### Manual installation (developer mode)

1. Clone this repository
2. Open `chrome://extensions/` in Chrome
3. Enable "Developer mode" (top right)
4. Click "Load unpacked" and select the extension folder
